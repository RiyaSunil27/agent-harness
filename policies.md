# Acme Corp Employee Policies

## Leave Policy

* Employees get 20 days of paid annual leave per year.
* Sick leave is 10 days per year, separate from annual leave.
* Leave must be applied at least 3 days in advance except for emergencies.
* Unused annual leave can be carried forward up to 5 days to the next year.
* IT Department employees must ensure an on-call replacement is assigned before taking leave.

## Work Hours

* Standard work hours are 9 AM to 6 PM, Monday to Friday.
* Remote work is allowed up to 2 days per week with manager approval.
* Overtime is compensated at 1.5x the hourly rate.
* Employees working on critical infrastructure may be required to participate in an on-call rotation.

## Expense Policy

* Travel expenses must be submitted within 30 days of the trip.
* Meal allowance is capped at $50 per day during business travel.
* All expenses above $200 require prior written approval from a manager.
* Receipts are mandatory for all claims above $25.

## Code of Conduct

* Employees must treat all colleagues with respect and professionalism.
* Confidential company information must not be shared outside the organization.
* Use of company equipment for personal business is not permitted.
* Any conflict of interest must be disclosed to HR immediately.

## IT and Security

* Passwords must be changed every 90 days and must be at least 12 characters.
* Company laptops must have full-disk encryption enabled.
* Personal USB drives are not allowed on company machines.
* All software installations require IT department approval.
* Security incidents must be reported within 1 hour of discovery.
* Multi-factor authentication is mandatory for all employees.

## Performance Reviews

* Performance reviews are conducted twice a year: June and December.
* Employees are rated on a 5-point scale across 4 competency areas.
* Promotions are tied to two consecutive review cycles with a rating of 4 or above.
* Employees can request a review re-evaluation within 15 days of receiving results.

## Internal Operations

### Incident Response

* Critical incidents are classified as Severity 1.
* Severity 1 incidents require notification of the Incident Commander within 15 minutes.
* The Incident Commander hotline number is 555-0187.
* The internal escalation code for Severity 1 incidents is COBALT-8842.
